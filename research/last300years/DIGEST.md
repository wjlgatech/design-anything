# /last300years — civilizational survival test (1726–2026)

Inclusion rule: multi-generational, still-in-use evidence (taught in accredited
curricula, encoded in current standards/codes, or actively practiced in 2026).
Whig-history guard: influential-but-superseded ideas are flagged; their
surviving kernel is kept, the dogma discarded.

Each entry ends with **AI-gate**: how the principle constrains an AI blueprint
generator. These feed `principles/DESIGN_PRINCIPLES.md` and the ready-gate roadmap.

## Architecture

1. **Vitruvius — firmitas/utilitas/venustas** (c. 25 BCE; the root). Still the opening framework of first-year architecture worldwide; ISO usability's effectiveness/efficiency/satisfaction is its descendant. **AI-gate:** three parallel validators — structural, functional, aesthetic — and fail if any one is zero.
2. **Proportion systems** (Palladio 1570 → parametric facades). Ratio *systems* survive because consistent modules aid coordination — the golden ratio's perceptual magic is weakly supported. **AI-gate:** snap dimensions to one consistent ratio/module family; never arbitrary floats.
3. **Sullivan — form follows function** (1896). 130 years as the most-quoted design maxim; survived the postmodern attack on it. **AI-gate:** derive geometry from the functional program (adjacencies, loads, circulation) first; never style-first.
4. **Bauhaus** (1919–1933). The Vorkurs still templates design-school first years; Bauhaus-lineage products in continuous production 90+ years. **AI-gate:** outputs must be manufacturable by the declared machine (printer envelope, material, tolerances) — a hard gate, not a preference.
5. **Le Corbusier — Modulor kernel** (1948). Verbatim Modulor is dead; its kernel — anthropometric dimensioning — is now *law* (ADA, ISO 21542). His urbanism (towers-in-park) is a documented failure mode. **AI-gate:** reachable/passable dimensions derive from body-data tables, not aesthetics. Keep the ruler, discard the city.
6. **Christopher Alexander — A Pattern Language** (1977). Never out of print in 49 years; spawned software patterns (GoF) and the wiki; arguably the most AI-relevant design book ever. **AI-gate:** the generator's core representation should be a composable pattern library (context/problem/solution) — Alexander pre-specified the architecture of a blueprint generator.
7. **Vernacular tradition** (Rudofsky 1964 → Passivhaus 1991). Climate-optimal, material-local solutions evolved over centuries; codified in passive-design standards. **AI-gate:** condition on climate zone + local materials; retrieve vernacular precedent before proposing form.
8. **Howard — Garden Cities** (1898). Letchworth still functions; green belts are live planning law. **AI-gate:** reserve open-space ratios as structural, not residual.
9. **Jane Jacobs** (1961). Overturned urban renewal and stayed overturned; her four conditions live in 2020s zoning reform. **AI-gate:** score neighborhood outputs on mix-of-use, block permeability, street activation — never optimize one building in a void.
10. **Feng shui & non-Western spatial canons**. Actively applied in 2026 East-Asian markets; the defensible kernel (south orientation, sheltered entries, no door-window wind tunnels) converges with passive design. **AI-gate:** expose a user-selectable cultural-convention constraint layer, distinct from physics.
11. **Biophilic design** (Olmsted 1858 → Wilson 1984 → WELL/LEED). Ulrich's 1984 hospital-window study replicated repeatedly. **AI-gate:** daylight access, nature views, prospect/refuge geometry scored on every habitable room.

## Interior & furniture

12. **Shaker design** (1780s–1860s). "Beauty rests on utility"; reproduced 200+ years; documented influence on Danish modern and 2020s minimalism. **AI-gate:** prefer integrated storage and self-explaining joinery; ornament justifies itself or is omitted.
13. **Thonet No. 14** (1859). 6 parts, 10 screws, 36 chairs/m³ — first flat-pack; still in production (TON "214"); ancestor of IKEA and every DFM curriculum. **AI-gate:** minimize part count, design for disassembly/shipping, repeat identical components.
14. **Eames** (1940s–70s). "The best for the most for the least"; pieces in continuous production 70+ years; the prototype-test-iterate process is canon. **AI-gate:** bake iteration in — generate, simulate against body models, refine; never single-shot.
15. **Wabi-sabi / ma / Scandinavian modern**. 400+ years of continuous Japanese practice; ma explicitly taught globally. **AI-gate:** negative space is budgeted deliberately; allow materials that age visibly.
16. **Neufert (1936) & Panero-Zelnik (1979) anthropometrics**. Neufert: ~43 editions, 20+ languages, 90 years in print — likely the most-consulted architecture reference on Earth. **AI-gate:** these tables ARE the hard-constraint database; a layout violating Neufert-class clearances is a *bug*. The single most directly implementable item on this list.

## Craft → industrial → digital manufacturing

17. **Interchangeable parts** (Gribeauval 1760s → Springfield Armory → ASME Y14.5 GD&T). 200 years unbroken. **AI-gate:** explicit tolerances per process; mating parts checked across tolerance stack-up, not nominals.
18. **Standardization — DIN (1917) / ISO (1947)**; A-series paper (1922) as the purest case. Governs threads, drawings, BIM (ISO 19650), 3D-print formats (3MF). **AI-gate:** emit standard fasteners/stock by default; nonstandard requires explicit override.
19. **Modular coordination** (ISO 2848 100mm module; tatami as the 400-year proof). Underlies masonry, drywall, 600mm kitchen module. **AI-gate:** default to a declared planning grid; count and minimize off-module dimensions.
20. **Prefab's paired lesson** — Crystal Palace (1851) + Sears kit homes (~70,000, tens of thousands still standing) vs **Lustron** (failed at ~2,500): open-system prefab survives, closed proprietary systems die. **AI-gate:** 3D-printed construction must interface with conventional trades — print the walls, buy the windows.
21. **DFMA → DfAM** (Thonet → Model T → Boothroyd-Dewhurst 1980s → additive rules today). 45° overhangs, min wall thickness, anisotropy govern 2026 printing. **AI-gate:** a per-process rule engine (FDM vs SLA vs concrete extrusion) applied *during* generation, not as post-hoc rejection.

## Game / play design roots

22. **Huizinga — Homo Ludens** (1938). The magic circle structures Salen & Zimmerman (2004), still the standard text; 88 years unreplaced. **AI-gate:** generated play spaces need explicit boundaries, entry/exit rituals, internally consistent rules.
23. **Caillois — agôn/alea/mimicry/ilinx** (1958). 65+ years as the standard play taxonomy. **AI-gate:** take the intended play-type mix as an input parameter — an ilinx space and an agôn arena have opposite geometry requirements.
24. **Chess (~1,400y) and Go (~2,500y) — the deep-time controls**. Minimal orthogonal rules, inexhaustible state space; Go's rules fit an index card yet resisted AI until 2016. **AI-gate:** optimize rule *economy*; measure depth/complexity ratio.
25. **Why play systems survive** (+ Sid Meier's "interesting decisions"). Cheap equipment, orally transmissible rules, balanced starts, decision density at every skill level. **AI-gate:** validate transmissibility (rule-text length), fairness (first-move advantage bounds), decision density via self-play.

## Enduring meta-principles

26. **Repairability / shearing layers** (Japanese demountable joinery → Brand 1994 → EU right-to-repair). 1,000-year temples survive *because* layers separate. **AI-gate:** separate structure/skin/services/space-plan as independently replaceable layers; prefer mechanical fastening — the critical caution for monolithic 3D-printed walls.
27. **Human scale** (Sitte 1889 → Gehl 1971). Gehl's metrics transformed Copenhagen; medieval streets have satisfied users for 700 years. **AI-gate:** evaluate every design at eye level (1.6m) at walking speed, not god-view axonometric.
28. **Daylight as a right** ("ancient lights" 1663 → EN 17037 → WELL). **AI-gate:** sun-path/daylight-autonomy simulation per habitable room as a hard gate; windows derived from orientation, never decorative.
29. **Thermal mass & passive control** (Taos Pueblo ~1,000y → Passivhaus, 35 years, tens of thousands certified). Printed earth/concrete walls are natural thermal mass. **AI-gate:** co-optimize mass, glazing, orientation, shading against climate data before sizing HVAC.
30. **Courtyard typology** (Ur c. 2000 BCE → domus → siheyuan → riad → 2020s revival). Possibly the longest continuously used plan type (~4,000 years) — solves light/air/privacy/security/microclimate in one move. **AI-gate:** courtyard/atrium/lightwell as first-class partis — a strong prior for deep plans.

## Cross-cutting verdicts

1. **The most implementable survivors are tables and grids, not slogans**: anthropometrics (#16), modular coordination (#19), DfAM rules (#21), daylight simulation (#28) convert straight into hard constraints.
2. **Alexander's pattern language (#6) pre-specifies this repo's architecture** — it already survived one substrate change (buildings → software); strong evidence it can cross another (software → AI generation).
3. **The failure archive matters**: Lustron (#20) and Corbusian urbanism (#5) are the anti-patterns — closed proprietary systems and context-free single-object optimization are the two classic ways generated designs die.
4. **Whig-guard summary**: golden-ratio mysticism and verbatim Modulor did not survive scrutiny; consistent proportion systems and anthropometric dimensioning did. Feng shui survives as living cultural practice plus a passive-design kernel — model it as a convention layer, not physics.
